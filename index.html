A Letter for Miss Maske

June 14, 2025

To Miss Maske

My dearest Nayanti,

I find myself often lost in thought and most often, those thoughts are of thee. Strange, is it not? How a friendship so simple hath woven itself into something far more tender.

When I look upon thee, I see not just a friend, but a soul that feels like home. Thy laughter lightens my days, and thy words linger with me long after we part.

What I feel for thee is no passing fancy, but something true and steadfast. A quiet flame that hath grown with every moment we've shared.

If thou dost feel even a fragment of what stirs in me, I would be honored to walk further down this path, hand in hand with thee.

With all sincerity,
Shiva Shrestha
